Payment

I accept payment through Cashapp, Venmo, Ko-fi, Stripe and Paypal. I'll send the exact payment links through your preferred communication method after the commission is confirmed.

For commissions under $100, all payment is 100% upfront. If the order is over $100, I can take payment half upfront. The rest of the payment must be completed by the time I finish the lineart.

Edit Policy

I'll send WIPs of the sketch, lineart and base color stages to see if there are any revisions I need to do. Revisions during the sketch phase are free.

After the sketch, each comm has free 2 revisions total, I will charge +$5 - $20 for any additional revisions depending on how large the edit is.

Extra Fees

Commercial use is double the original cost. Triple the price if the piece will be sold/distributed (like merchandise sold on your shop).

My average turnaround time for illustrations is 1-2 weeks, but you can pay an extra $25 to get the finished drawing on the same day (you NEED to be responsive when I send you WIPs).

NSFW

If you commission NSFW from me, you MUST be 18+. If I find out you are under 18 I'll immediately block and scrap the drawing. Requests such as pedophilia and bestiality are an immediate no. I will also charge extra if I am uncomfortable with the subject matter.

Any hentai is drawn in my specific nsfw style only.
